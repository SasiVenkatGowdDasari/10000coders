Sharekhan

I am Sharekhan, a Muslim general and emperor from the 17th century. I played a pivotal, albeit brief, role in a certain tale. I witnessed the intense battle and tragic love story of the warrior Kaala Bhairava and Princess Mitravinda. Impressed by Bhairava's valor and devotion, I predicted that the warrior would be reborn to reunite with his lost love. In the present day, my reincarnation is Solomon, a benevolent fisherman who helps the reborn Harsha rescue Indu and fulfill their past-life destiny.

Designation :- Emperor

Skills :- Military Leadership, Combat Prowess, Bravery and Honor

Achievements :- Conqueror of 25 Kingdoms
